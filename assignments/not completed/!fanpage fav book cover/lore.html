Discovery of Identity

Percy finds out he’s a demigod — the son of Poseidon — and learns about the world of Greek gods, monsters, and Camp Half-Blood.

The Quest for the Lightning Bolt

Percy goes on his first big quest to find Zeus’s stolen lightning bolt and prevent a war between the gods.

Rise of Kronos

An ancient Titan, Kronos, starts gaining power again. Percy and his friends try to stop his followers from bringing him back.

The Great Prophecy

A mysterious prophecy says a child of the Big Three will decide the fate of Olympus. Percy learns it might be about him.

The Battle for Olympus

Kronos returns by taking over a demigod’s body. Percy and his friends must defend New York and stop Kronos from destroying Olympus.

Choosing Your Own Fate

Percy rejects the offer to become a god and instead chooses a normal life, helping build a better future for all demigods.
