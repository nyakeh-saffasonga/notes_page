1: The Lightning Thief (2005)

Percy Jackson discovers he is a demigod, the son of Poseidon, and embarks on a quest to prevent a war among the gods. The story introduces the world of Greek mythology intertwined with the modern world, featuring Percy’s adventures at Camp Half-Blood and his fight against mythological monsters.

2: The Sea of Monsters (2006)

Percy and his friends set out to find the Golden Fleece in the Sea of Monsters to save Camp Half-Blood. The quest deepens Percy’s connection to his divine heritage and introduces new mythological creatures while confronting the growing threat of the Titan Kronos.

3: The Titan's Curse (2007)

Percy embarks on a dangerous quest to rescue Artemis, the goddess of the hunt, and Annabeth, who has been kidnapped. The story introduces new mythological figures and reveals deeper prophecies about Percy’s destiny and the looming threat of Kronos.

4: The Battle of the Labyrinth (2008)

Percy and his friends navigate the Labyrinth to stop Kronos from invading Camp Half-Blood. The story explores Greek mythology in a maze filled with danger, secrets, and new allies, setting the stage for the final confrontation with Kronos.

5: The Last Olympian (2009)

Percy faces his greatest challenge as Kronos’s army attacks Olympus. The novel culminates in Percy’s decisive battle to protect the gods and the mortal world, revealing his true destiny and the future of the demigod world.

6: Wrath of the Triple Goddess (2022)

This novel explores new mythological threats and challenges for Percy and his friends, delving into the complex relationships among the gods, demigods, and monsters as they face fresh dangers and uncover deeper secrets of the Greek pantheon.

7: The Chalice of the Gods (2023)

Percy returns for a new adventure, balancing school, friendships, and divine quests. He must find the legendary chalice to prevent a new threat from disrupting the delicate balance between gods and mortals.
