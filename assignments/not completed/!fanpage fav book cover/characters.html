Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is the main protagonist of the series. He is a demigod, the son of Poseidon, and has a strong sense of loyalty and bravery.

Annabeth Chase

Annabeth Chase is a demigod, the daughter of Athena, and is known for her intelligence and strategic thinking. She is Percy's close friend and love interest.

Grover Underwood

Grover Underwood is a satyr and Percy's best friend. He is loyal and protective, often going to great lengths to ensure Percy's safety.

Luke Castellan

Luke Castellan is a demigod, the son of Hermes, who initially appears as a friend but later becomes an antagonist in the series.

Clarisse La Rue

Clarisse La Rue is a fierce and brave demigod daughter of Ares. She is known for her fiery temper and combat skills. She often clashes with Percy but shows loyalty to her friends.

Chiron

Chiron is a wise centaur and trainer of heroes at Camp Half-Blood. He serves as a mentor to Percy and other demigods, guiding them through their quests and training.
