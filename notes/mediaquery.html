Responsive Device Layout

grid below from 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 column @480px, @768px and @1024px

XS: under 480px

single column. prioritize readability and tap targets

SM: >= 480px < 768

two columns. cards can sit side-by-side comfortably

MD >= 768 <= 1024px

three columns. more content per row. increased whitespace or gamespace

LG >= 1024px < 1280px

four columns. add more breathing room and option ul

orientation

use portrait or landscape: these do require layout tweaks

accessibility

disable animations, keep color changes to a minimum

color schemes

make your color schemes with light and dark mode in mind

tips

keep breakpoint few and meaningful, design content first, mobile layout first/p>
